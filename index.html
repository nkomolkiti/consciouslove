Words can deeply hurt and lower a child's self esteem and worth, especially from loved ones. Verbal Abuse can be quiet, repetitive, and hurt either intentionally or unintentionally. The impacts are less obvious, and most time become 'normal' in the child's perspective.

Being a new parent, it can be stressful to take care of a child. Conscious Love is the school that provides an online course called 'The Insignificant Word' that aims to provide a better understanding of verbal abuse, enlighten any misconceptions and provide tips to positive parenting.

This course is not a campaign, a verdict, or an absolute solution. By practicing positive parenting, the family relationship becomes stronger, resulting in a happier upbringing and positive impact on children.
